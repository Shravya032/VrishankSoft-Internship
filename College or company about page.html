About Our College- SMVITM

Founded in 2010 by H. H. Shri Vishwavallabha Theertha Swamiji of Shri Sode Vadiraja Mutt, Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology & Management (SMVITM) has quickly risen to prominence as one of the most promising engineering and management colleges in coastal Karnataka. Located in Bantakal, Udupi, SMVITM blends spiritual roots with academic excellence, offering value-based, industry-aligned technical and management education to students from all backgrounds. Affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), approved by AICTE, and recognized by the Government of Karnataka, the institute offers B.E. programs in emerging and core disciplines including Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. In its latest academic expansion, SMVITM established a full-fledged Department of Management Studies, in 2023, offering a 2-year MBA program under VTU, with dual specializations in Business Analytics, HR, Finance and Marketing, designed to meet modern industry and entrepreneurial demands.
