Water Pollution Awareness

Water pollution is a critical environmental issue that affects ecosystems, human health, and the overall quality of life. It occurs when harmful substances, such as chemicals, waste, and microorganisms, contaminate water bodies like rivers, lakes, and oceans. This webpage aims to raise awareness about the various sources of water pollution and their detrimental effects on the environment.

Purpose: This webpage uses images and interactive elements to visually demonstrate different sources of water pollution and their impact on the environment.
